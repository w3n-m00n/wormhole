Trade to Farm.

Whenever you buy or sell a minimum of 50 WHOLE, you will receive airdrops from the next 5 transactions that happen after yours! You get higher airdrop rewards if you buy rather than sell.

Tokenomics

  • Initial total supply: 100,000 WHOLE (60% presale, 40% liquidity)
  • Each transaction has a 6% fee:
    • 4% goes to farmers
    • 1% goes to marketing
    • 1% is burnt forever

Weekly Events

Event Horizon

24-hour event allowing community members to vote to decrease the minimum $WHOLE required to be part of the farming/airdrop slot when they trade.

Singularity

24-hour event allowing community members to vote to increase the minimum $WHOLE required to be part of the farming/airdrop slot when they trade.

Space Farming

During this event, 500 tokens from the Dev fees wallet are sold and bought back repeatedly until they get depleted. This gives traders a chance to get massive airdrop rewards!

Asteroid Mining

2 hour event where every 10 minutes, 5 farmers will be locked into the farming slot. They will consistently receive airdrops for next 10 minutes before the lock opens and re-locks again.
