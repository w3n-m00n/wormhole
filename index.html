How it works:

Wormhole is a first of its kind trade to farm DeFi protocol!

Any token holder that buys or sells on Uniswap is automatically added to a farming slot. Whilst in the farming slot they receive airdrops of Wormhole tokens for the next 5 consecutive transactions.

Ex: Farmer 1

Joe buys 12 Wormhole, his wallet is added to farming slot#1 to receive airdrop.

Ex: Farmer 2

Jacob also buys 14 wormhole, he pays fees (to Joe and others in the slot) and his wallet is added to #1 faming slot, Joe's wallet moves to #2 slot.
